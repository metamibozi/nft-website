About the Collection

The Rise of CONSECTRA collection represents a groundbreaking fusion of ancient mythology and digital innovation. Each of the 65 unique Phoenix NFTs embodies the eternal cycle of rebirth, resilience, and transformation.

Created by the CONSECTRA design team, this collection features:

  • ✓ 65 meticulously crafted digital artworks
  • ✓ Polygon Blockchain (ERC-1155 Standard)
  • ✓ 5% Royalty on all secondary sales
  • ✓ IPFS-based decentralized storage
  • ✓ Seven distinct rarity levels
  • ✓ Professional metadata standards

Each Phoenix tells a unique story of transformation, from the common "Celestial Phoenix" to the ultra-rare "Mythic" variants. The collection draws inspiration from global mythological traditions while embracing modern blockchain technology.
